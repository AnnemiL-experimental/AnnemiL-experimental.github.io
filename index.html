关于 About

Born in Xi'an, Shaanxi. Started in philosophy, then pivoted to engineering. Now finishing a B.S. in Biomedical Engineering at Miami University, working across tissue optics, behavioral neuroscience, and embedded biosignal processing systems.

Decades of living with psychiatric illness led me toward a single question: how do we physically intervene in mental suffering? Next step is graduate work in psychiatric brain-computer interfaces at Mount Sinai, pursuing multimodal physical therapies for psychiatric conditions.

Outside the lab, I make things. Drawing, painting, calligraphy, photography, writing. I watch too many films and listen to too much music. I play video games late at night.

Mn Removal from Potable Water

Investigated adsorption kinetics using activated biomass for low-cost water treatment. Conducted pyrolysis, BET surface-area analysis, TGA, colorimetry, and pH optimization. Team achieved >90% manganese removal efficiency.

Materials Testing: Silicone & Porcine Tissue

Characterized nonlinear hyperelastic and viscoelastic behavior of silicone rubber and porcine skeletal muscle under uniaxial tension and compression. Fitted four-term Ogden model (R² = 0.997).
